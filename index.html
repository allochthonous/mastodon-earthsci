Where are all the History and/or Philosophy of Science (HPS) scholars on Mastodon?

This page provides a list of HPS accounts and a way to follow multiple accounts at once. You can decide if you want to follow some or all of the accounts in the list. You are provided with a .csv file, which you can upload in Mastodon in order to follow those you select. Here you can find brief instructions on how to do this. You can follow and post HPS-related content using several hashtags: #histsci #philsci #HPS #histodons (for general history) #philosophy (for general philosophy)
